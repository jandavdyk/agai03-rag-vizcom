How long is the demo?
30 minutes. We keep it focused and respect your time.
Will this be a sales pitch?
No. We'll show you how Vizcom works, answer your questions honestly, and help you figure out if it's the right fit. If it's not, we'll tell you.
Can I see features specific to my industry?
Absolutely. That's why we ask about your industry and use case — every demo is tailored to what matters most to you.
What if I'm not ready to buy?
That's completely fine. The demo is about education, not commitment. Many people book demos just to understand how Vizcom works before making any decisions.
Who will run the demo?
Someone from our product expert or sales team who knows Vizcom inside and out — and who can answer technical, implementation, and pricing questions on the spot.
Can I bring my team?
Yes! We encourage it. The more perspectives, the better the conversation.
What happens after the demo?
If Vizcom feels like a fit, we can set up a trial for your team right away. If not, no hard feelings — we're here to help you find the right solution, even if it's not us.
