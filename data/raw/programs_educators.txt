Ask a question
Who qualifies for education access?
Currently enrolled post secondary student at accredited institutions and post-secondary faculty/educators teaching design. You'll need a .edu email and proof of enrollment or employment.
What's included in the education plan?
The Vizcom Education plan includes 500/month Modify Pro generations, 50/month Animations, 100/month 3D generations — full details on our pricing page.
Educators get access to a free Vizcom Workspace.
How long does access last?
Students have access as long as they're enrolled. Access renews annually with re-verification.
Is there really no cost?
Yes, it's completely free for verified students. As long as you’re a student, the plan is free.
How long does verification take?
Most applications are approved within 24-72 hours. If you have a .edu email, the process is usually faster.
Can I use Vizcom for commercial projects?
The education license is for learning and coursework. Students can include their work in portfolios. For commercial use, you'll need a professional license after graduation.
Do I need to re-verify?
Yes, annually. We'll send a reminder when it's time to renew your education access.
What if my school doesn't have .edu emails?
No problem. You can verify with a student ID, enrollment letter, or other proof of enrollment/employment. Just contact us and we'll help.
