Prompt
Reimagine a pair of mules in Vizcom. Designers are invited to rethink this familiar form, exploring bold ideas, unexpected forms, and expressive details. Focus on clarity of concept while considering how your design could translate to the real world.

Constraints
No boot, low-top or high-top designs.
No open toe designs.
Must be 3D printable. No mixed material designs.
Avoid cutouts (ie. holes) on the body of your mule.

Challenge stages
Stage 1: Design your mule in Vizcom
Participants have
two weeks
to design and submit their mule designs. Mules must be designed in Vizcom and must meet the submission guidelines to qualify for judging.
‍
Stage 2: Judging and evaluation
Using the judging criteria below, the Vizcom and Zellerfeld team will shortlist and announce the top 30 submissions.
Our judging panel (of industry footwear designers) will then select the top 3 submissions that will move on to production with Zellerfeld.
Note: Decisions cannot be appealed. Winners will be selected based on the judges' collective assessment.
‍
Stage 3: Production
The top 3 submissions will 3D modeling their designs to meet the Zellerfeld production guidelines. Designers will submit the required files directly in Zellerfeld Studio and pass their auto-checks.
The Vizcom and Zellerfeld team will provide  assistance here as needed. 3D modelling knowledge is not necessary, but is a bonus.
‍
Stage 4: Wrap up
The Zellerfeld team will produce and ship samples of your shoe once complete. Vizcom will work directly with the winners to produce spotlight content.

Judging Criteria
Submissions will be evaluated by a panel of judges based on the following criteria.
Creativity & Originality (35%)
Uniqueness of the concept, strength of the idea, and how compellingly the design reimagines the mule.
Design Quality (25%)
Overall execution, attention to detail, form, proportion, and clarity of the final presentation.
Process (10%)
How effectively Vizcom was used throughout the creative process, including iteration, exploration, and workflow development.
Feasibility (20%)
Consideration of real-world production, materials, and how the design could translate from concept to physical product.
Alignment with Brief (10%)
How well the design meets the challenge guidelines and constraints.

Ask a question
Can I submit multiple designs?
Yes, you can submit up to 3 designs per challenge.
Do I need a paid Vizcom account to enter?
No. Free accounts are eligible to participate. The free plan includes:
- 75/month Image Generation
- 25/month Image & Upscale Enhancement
- 25/month Background Removal Each account is also automatically topped up with 2,250 premium action credits which roughly translates to:
-75 Modify Pro (custom prompts) or
-18 Animations or
-37 Make 3D Model Participates on a free plan are encouraged to be mindful of how much you use each feature so you can allocate your limits towards what's needed for the final submission 📥
Who owns my design after I submit it?
You do. You retain full ownership. By submitting, you just give us permission to showcase your work in connection with the challenge.
What happens if my design wins?
We'll manufacture it and ship it to you! You'll also be featured on our website and social channels. You keep the rights to your design.
Can I use my winning design in my portfolio or sell it?
Absolutely. You retain all rights to your work.
How are winners chosen?
A panel of judges from Vizcom and our partners will evaluate submissions based on creativity, design quality, feasibility, and alignment with the brief.
When will I know if I won?
Winners will be announced 30 days after the challenge kicks off and notified via email within 48 hours.
Can I enter if I don't live in the US?
Yes! The challenge is open worldwide to anyone 18 or older.
Can I update my submission after I've entered?
No. Submissions are final once submitted. Make sure you're happy with your design before you hit submit.
I have another question. Who do I contact?
Join our Discord and head into our public #zellerfeld channel — we're happy to help!
